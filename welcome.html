Two Powerful Apps, One Membership

Trip Buddy and Food Buddy work together to keep your travel costs low and your journeys smooth.

🗺️ Trip Buddy — Road Intelligence

Trip Buddy is your real-time co-pilot on the road. Before you leave and while you drive, you get live intelligence on everything that matters for a safe, affordable, and enjoyable journey.

  • Real-time gas prices — find the cheapest fuel stations along your route
  • City Dispatch Reports — crime alerts, weather, road hazards, and danger zones ahead
  • Places to Stay & Eat — top-rated hotels, motels, restaurants, and local favorites
  • Hospital & Urgent Care locator — peace of mind wherever you travel
  • Electric vehicle charging stations along your route
  • Pet-friendly rest areas for travelers with animals
  • Budget-friendly road recipes for meals on the go
  • Downloadable PDF route report — take your intelligence offline

🥦 Food Buddy — Grocery Intelligence

Food Buddy tracks grocery prices across the major stores in your area so you always know where to get the best deal. Shop smarter before you ever leave home.

  • Weekly price comparison across Walmart, WinCo, Fred Meyer, Safeway, and Grocery Outlet
  • Basket total comparison — see which store wins the week
  • Savings calculator — know exactly how much you save vs. the most expensive store
  • Budget recipes using this week's best-priced ingredients
  • Food Buddy News — stay updated on grocery trends and food price alerts
